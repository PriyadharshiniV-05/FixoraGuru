How FixoraGuru Works

your request

We Find

They Fix

Step 1

Choose a service

Request an electrician, plumber, heating and gas engineer, handyperson, or something else-er. We provide you with a fixed price per hour at the point of booking so you exactly what you’ll pay for their services.

Step 2

Tell us where and when you need your Fixer

Enter your address and choose the date you’d like your Fixer to come round and do their thing. You can specify morning or afternoon slots 7 days a week for maximum flexibility.

Step 3

Share a few details about your job

Share a little about the task at hand to help us send the right Fixer for the job. If possible, take a few pictures of the problem and upload them along with your description.

Step 4

Let us take care of the rest.

We match you with a trusted, fully qualified JustFix tradesperson in your local area and arrange for them to visit at your specified time. Not a cowboy in sight; just reputable professionals with the experience and skills needed to get the job done right.
